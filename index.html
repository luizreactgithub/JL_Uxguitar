Styles

Hard Rock

Hard rock is characterized by an emphasis on bluesy guitar riffs, powerful vocals, and heavy, thunderous drums. In order to understand what makes a subgenre like hard rock meaningful, we must understand the other genres that contrast it.

Heavy Metal

Heavy metal is traditionally characterized by loud distorted guitars, emphatic rhythms, dense bass-and-drum sound and vigorous vocals. Heavy metal subgenres variously emphasize, alter or omit one or more of these attributes.
